COVID Lung Detector

Created by Kevin, Sam, Justin

Using a public dataset, Keras to create a neural network and machine learning from Tensorflow, this program was create to recognizes the 2019 Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) also known as the Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2) in X-ray images of human lungs. In hopes of further assisting with the battle with the global pandemic, this site was created for the users to determine if the images of lungs they have belong to an individual who are showing developement of COVID.
